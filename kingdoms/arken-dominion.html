Arken Dominion

Status

Destroyed

The Arken Dominion no longer exists as a sovereign nation.

Overview

The Arken Dominion was once a theocratic empire fueled by divine zealotry, volcanic industry, and the legacy of the dragon hunts.

It ceased to function as a nation following the Cataclysm at Varkhaz-Thul, when a single mage unleashed a ninth-level spell of catastrophic scale upon the Dominion’s heartland.

The Cataclysm at Varkhaz-Thul

Witnesses reported the appearance of a second star above the Dominion shortly before the destruction began.

Moments later, Varkhaz-Thul and much of the surrounding territory were consumed by an arcane impact that shattered the capital, broke the central government, and scattered the Dominion Legions.

The exact spell used has not been confirmed, but the event has been classified by surviving scholars as a ninth-level arcane catastrophe.
