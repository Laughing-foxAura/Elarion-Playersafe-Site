Thalor Sylvae

Dream Archive

This record no longer remains in the same form between viewings.

Multiple revisions exist simultaneously.

Overview

Thalor Sylvae is remembered.

Its borders are not.

The forest appears unchanged across every surviving map.

The paths through it do not.

Culture & Society

The oldest copies describe harmony.

Newer copies omit entire centuries.

One version claims no cities were ever built.

Another insists the trees remember them all.

The Sleeping Queen

The Queen continues to dream.

No record disputes this.

Everything written after that begins to diverge.

Some accounts describe a sleeping monarch.

Others describe someone who has already awakened.

A few insist she has always been speaking.

No scholar has successfully determined which version was written first.

The forest still refuses unwanted visitors.

Relationships

Diplomatic records have become dreamlike.
