Dravandor Confederacy

Overview

The Dravandor Confederacy is a union of five independent city-states that formed a defensive alliance against the growing power of Virelia. Though united in name, each city maintains its own identity, laws, and leadership. Internal tensions and ideological differences prevent full cohesion, but the shared threat of invasion keeps them aligned.

  • Type: Confederacy of free cities
  • Structure: Semi-independent city-states with a rotating High Council
  • Capital (rotating): Currently Myrhold
  • Military: Diverse and decentralized; relies on regional militias and contracted adventurers
  • Magic: Lightly regulated; varies by city
  • Alignment: True Neutral to Chaotic Good

Member Cities

Myrhold (Current Capital)

  • Known for its strategic location and strong walls
  • Hosts the rotating High Council

Tarnath

  • A coastal trade hub with a vast black market
  • Major port for smuggling and privateers

Velmira

  • Home to bards, artists, and illusionists
  • Politically rebellious, culturally vibrant

Cenwyn

  • Deep inland city surrounded by ruins
  • Recently uncovered relics from Selune

Government

  • Each city is ruled by a local council or elected archon
  • High Council meets twice per season; rotates by city
  • Laws vary per city but Confederacy-wide rules protect member sovereignty and mutual defense

Magic and Adventurers

  • Magic is freely practiced in most cities, though Cenwyn now places restrictions near rift zones
  • The Confederacy has close ties with the Adventurer’s Guild
  • Local mages from Obelion often visit for research or diplomacy

Military

  • No standing army
  • Each city maintains its own militia
  • Hires adventurers and mercenaries for major campaigns
  • War with Virelia is costly; most fighting takes place near Myrhold and Tarnath

Relations

  • Virelia – At war
  • Myriath – Spiritual ally, but politically distant
  • Obelion – Valued for magic and healing aid
  • Valewind Pactlands – Trade partner; seen as unreliable in war
