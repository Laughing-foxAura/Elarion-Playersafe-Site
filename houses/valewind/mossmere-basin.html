Mossmere Basin

A wide, fertile lowland where the leyline pulses beneath tranquil fields and flowing waters.

Overview

Nestled in a broad, mist-shrouded depression in the eastern Valewind Pactlands, Mossmere Basin is a haven of herbal craft, water-based magic, and elemental communion. The region is lush year-round, its waterways fed by underground aquifers that pulse with leyline resonance, giving rise to flora and fauna found nowhere else.

What appears at first glance to be an agrarian settlement is actually one of the most spiritually attuned enclaves in the Pactlands, where the boundaries between the Material Plane and the elemental forces of Water and Life grow thin.

Landscape & Life

  • Canals and terraces dominate the landscape, feeding shimmering rice paddies, medicinal herb beds, and strange floating lily platforms.
  • The air is thick with fragrance, from dew-heavy lavender to basil moss and pondblossoms.
  • Water elementals, called rillkin, wander freely — most the size of children, some the size of hills.
  • Certain trees and reeds hum or sing when touched by wind or rain, resonating with the leyline like living instruments.

People & Leadership

The basin is led by the Circle of the Bloomed Flow, a council of herbalists, spirit-tenders, and druids.

Mira Hollowroot
Current speaker of the council. An herbalist-diplomat who has studied at both Greenthorn Canopy and Obelion.

  • Serves as the bridge between the practical and spiritual aspects of Mossmere.
  • Calm, deliberate, and known for carrying live moss on her robes.

Traditions & Magic

  • Herbcraft is more than alchemy — it is a sacred rite, blending plant lore, song, and elemental cooperation.
  • Apprentices learn the Three Infusions:
    • Sunbrew – tied to warmth and growth
    • Dewsteep – tied to clarity and healing
    • Rootfold – tied to memory and stillness
  • Spirit festivals are held during the Flood Moon, when the wetlands are at their peak and the veil thins.

Role in the Pactlands

Mossmere provides:

  • The highest quality herbs and spiritual tonics in the region
  • Spiritual balancing services to enclaves with unstable magical zones
  • Peacekeeping rituals and mediating presence
  • A tradition of neutrality, avoiding direct confrontation but subtly shaping outcomes

Conflicts & Tensions

While peaceful, Mossmere is not without threat:

  • Obelion’s presence has strengthened the leyline, causing some water spirits to become erratic or territorial
  • Internal druidic sects debate whether to bind the leyline more tightly or let it flow freely
  • Suspected external herb thieves have weakened the balance in certain groves
