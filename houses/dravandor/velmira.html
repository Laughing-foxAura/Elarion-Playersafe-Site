Velmira

The City of Mists and Masks

Where illusion cloaks truth, and revolution is rehearsed like theater.

Overview

Velmira rests in the highlands west of Myrhold, nestled among rolling hills of wildflowers, drifting mist, and ethereal twilight. At first glance, it feels more like a dream than a city — its towers twist impossibly, plazas shift with the seasons, and its fog-draped streets conceal both stages and secrets.

Once a quiet haven for eladrin and fey-touched wanderers, Velmira has evolved into a cradle of artistry, illusion, and political unrest.

Predominant Race

Eladrin form the heart and soul of Velmira, their seasonal moods shaping the very architecture and rhythm of the city.

Each district resonates with a seasonal aspect:

  • Springlight — Passion, invention, youthful optimism
  • Summertide — Dueling academies, pride, and vibrant expression
  • Autumborne — Philosophy, melancholy, poetic subversion
  • Winterveil — Cold resolve, silent rebellion, and covert planning

Other residents include:

  • Changelings – Welcomed for their fluid nature and kinship with illusion
  • Half-elves – Many raised as full citizens of Velmira
  • Humans and gnomes – Often drawn by the city’s bardic academies and arcane salons

Government & Order

Culture & Magic

  • Illusion magic is everywhere — from streetlamps and signage to clothing and architecture
  • Velmirans speak in double meanings and wear enchanted masks or accessories that shift with emotional state
  • Theater is sacred — every major play is a political act, and subversion is applauded more than patriotism
  • Some streets only appear at night, or only to those in particular moods or memories

Economic Role

Velmira exports:

  • Enchantment and illusion services
  • Elite graduates from bardic and arcane academies
  • Cultural and theatrical works with magical or ideological weight

Technically illegal activities quietly tolerated:

  • Illusion-based gambling
  • Glamour duels
  • Fey-pact ceremonies

Current Tensions

  • Radical youth factions demand either independence or resistance to Obelion’s influence
  • A traveling troupe vanished last month; only their enchanted masks were recovered from Foglake
  • Illusory storms have begun sweeping the city unbidden — too complex for casual spellcasters, yet no mage claims credit
