Myrhold

The Roosted Bastion

Capital of the Pactlands, where claws, feathers, fur, and fangs defend the Pact.

Overview

Myrhold is a fortified, stone-clad city built atop a high ridge surrounded by deep canyons and fast rivers. It serves as the capital of the Valewind Pactlands and hosts the rotating High Council, where leaders from enclaves and member cities govern through consensus.

Unlike most capitals, Myrhold reflects no single race or culture. Instead, it is a communal stronghold, shaped by the needs and styles of its diverse inhabitants — especially those of animalistic and primal lineages.

You'll find:

  • High roosts for aarakocra
  • Burrows and heating tunnels engineered by kobolds
  • Tree-shaded avenues where harengon merchants and lizardfolk guards mingle
  • Open-air plazas for tabaxi dancers and shifter scouts

Predominant Races

  • Shifters – Versatile scouts, spies, and guards
  • Tabaxi – Traders, storytellers, performers, and messengers
  • Lizardfolk – Practical laborers, hydrological engineers, and stalwart militia
  • Kobolds – Tinkerers and civic planners; designed Myrhold’s tunnels and ventilation
  • Harengon – Ferry operators, agricultural leaders, and festival organizers
  • Aarakocra – Skyward sentinels and council couriers, with aeries along canyon cliffs

Government & Culture

  • The High Council convenes every 30 days in the Stone Nest, a massive open-air amphitheater
  • Council chairs rotate by consensus between enclaves and cities
  • The civil government is managed by a Warden Marshal, elected annually by council vote

Current Warden Marshal:
Skarn Emberplume — an aging aarakocra with a sharp mind and a deep distrust of Obelion

Myrhold values:

  • Coalition over conquest
  • Truth over diplomacy
  • Survival over tradition

Economy & Trade

While not a commercial hub like Tarnath, Myrhold thrives by:

  • Hosting diplomatic negotiations between distant groups
  • Maintaining elite defensive contracts and mercenary organizations
  • Operating sky-path courier networks and subterranean tunnel transport
  • Producing goods via kobold tinkerers and lizardfolk artisans

Military & Defense

Myrhold’s defense systems are legendary:

  • Multi-tiered walls, built with kobold ingenuity and shifter foresight
  • Roost towers used by aarakocra for dive support and long-range alerts
  • Hydraulic floodgates capable of redirecting canyon water during sieges
  • A diverse militia, where each racial community trains specialized units

The city is called the Roosted Bastion because it doesn’t just resist attack — it actively watches from every height and shadow.

Lore & Tensions

Despite its unity, Myrhold faces pressure:

  • Obelion’s presence continues to amplify the local leyline — some councilors fear it is reshaping the land itself
