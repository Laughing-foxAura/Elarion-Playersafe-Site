House Keldros

The Obsidian Architects

“Stone remembers flame.”

Overview

House Keldros is the silent cornerstone of the Arken Dominion — not born of fire or magic, but forged through generations of discipline, mastery, and design. They are builders of obsidian cities, architects of lava-fed aqueducts, and custodians of structural balance in a land shaped by eruption.

Their legacy is not flashy, but enduring. Where others shout prophecy or raise steel, Keldros lays stone, calculates pressure, and ensures the Dominion does not collapse under its own ambition.

Current Head

Mael Keldros
Architect-Prince of the Third Hollow
Renowned for designing six cities that have withstood both quakes and riftshock. Never once issued flawed schematics.

  • Speaks rarely, but his blueprints are considered gospel among Dominion engineers.
  • Dreams of “impossible geometries” — designs he claims are whispered from ancient stone.

Seat: Varthane Hollow

A subterranean stronghold carved directly into the obsidian crust below the capital, surrounded by molten rivers and basalt vaults.

  • Known for its unbreachable vault-doors.
  • Houses the Guild of Earthbinding and a deep-locked chamber where failed blueprints are ritually sealed.

Values & Pursuits

  • Greatness is built — not inherited, not gifted.
  • Magic is a tool, not a master. It must be wielded with precision.
  • The earth holds memory. All design should honor that memory.

Reputation Among Other Houses

  • Respected by House Varnoss for structural doctrine and siege planning.
  • Quietly distrusted by House Dravakar, who see their restraint as veiled dissent.
  • Frequently courted by House Ferrosyn for collaboration on lava-forging infrastructure.

Tools & Magical Approach

  • Uses enchanted tools — not spellcasters — to shape and stabilize architecture.
  • Devices like seismic gauges, stabilizing hammers, and lava-channeling rods are standard.
