House Branmar

Ash-Sealed Record

What was written here has been fired beyond reading

The page still smells faintly of coal, rain, and hot iron. No flame is visible.

Surviving Impression

“In fire, something answered.”

“The hammer fell three times. The fourth strike was not ours.”

All names, holdings, craft records, and alliances have burned cleanly out of the document.

Recovered Margins

The ink did not blacken. It retreated from the page as if the words had been too close to a furnace.

Pressed into the lower margin is a shallow mark, like a seal made by a heated thumb.

Do not reforge what has chosen to cool.
