Obelion

Overview

Obelion—known as the One True Tower—is the last unified mage tower in the world. Built on a floating island suspended by ancient magic, it is both sanctuary and laboratory for the most gifted arcanists. Obelion maintains neutrality in global conflicts, but its eyes are everywhere.

  • Type: Arcane Institution (Floating Island)
  • Leadership: The Circle of Five (rotating archmage council)
  • Goal: Advance magical knowledge and replicate the Perfect Barrier
  • Mobility: The island can move slowly over months
  • Alignment: Lawful Neutral

Structure & Geography

  • The floating island is anchored magically to the skies
  • Movement is slow and deliberate, requiring months to change regions
  • Currently resides above the Valewind Pactlands, where magic is tolerated and leylines are strong
Island Features
  • Skyward Hall – central tower and lecture spire
  • Leyforge – where leylines are bent into power sources
  • Vault of Mirrors – a library of reflection magic and prophecy
  • Astral Garden – a floating crystal dome for planar study
  • Windstep Docks – suspended platforms where airships or guests arrive

Magical Influence

Obelion radiates strong arcane energy that empowers spells cast nearby. The floating island’s position affects leylines in the region below.

Leyline Effects

The Tower’s presence is strengthening the central leyline of the Pactlands. Druids are divided:

  • Some fear destabilization if Obelion leaves
  • Others warn that this concentration of magic might awaken forgotten powers

Goals & Interests

  • Replicating the Perfect Barrier of Saint Vivi is a top priority
  • Study of rift energy, planar bleed, and remnants of Selune is ongoing
  • Accepts magically gifted individuals from all nations—status or origin does not matter

Political Stance

Obelion avoids war and refuses military contracts, maintaining total neutrality.

  • Virelia – Cordial but distant
  • Dravandor Confederacy – Occasionally aids with magical study
  • Myriath – Admired; seeks knowledge of the Barrier
  • Arken Dominion – Deeply distrusted due to anti-arcane beliefs
  • Thalor Sylvae – Holds mutual respect for ancient magics
  • Valewind Pactlands – Current host region; fragile balance with druids

Leadership: The Circle of Five

  • Archmages rotate by specialty and era. Current seats:
    • Archmage Veridan – Transmutation and leyline stabilization
    • High Seer Elora Vell – Divination and prophecy
    • Master Ghallor Tane – Planar studies and void defense
    • Scribe-Lord Kael of Chains – Forbidden magics and sealcraft
    • Mistress Aivenna of Glass – Illusions and diplomatic outreach

Adventure Hooks

  • A leyline surge below Obelion threatens a Pactland settlement—Obelion wants a team to investigate.
  • A mage has gone missing after researching the nature of the Barrier—last seen near Cenwyn in the Confederacy.
  • A rogue faction within Obelion seeks to descend the island permanently—help or stop them?
